Experience Learning

We believe in learning as a deeply personal activity.
Each student is unique and requires an individual approach.
To help learners and teachers to excel we provide a solution:
personalized and adaptive learning

Why gremloon

We looked at our own experiences as teachers and students and came to conclusion that alot is missing in current solutions. That's why we started gremloon - in an attempt to provide an opinionated, yet open and cost-effective approach to education.

What we provide

Identifying Knowledge Gaps

Our focus is on what we don't know but need to know to succeed with future tasks. We need to identify knowledge gaps and fill them. For that, we utilize the power of graphs to create an understanding of the contents.

Adaptive Learning

Every learner has individual needs and preferences. Through algorithmic decisionmaking, we aim to create efficient learning environments that match and adjust to the learners' needs.

Open Content

The Internet is filled with high-quality educational content. We believe in the reusability of existing resources, and provide tools for professionals to create unique learning paths for students from these resources.

Open Education

We see ourselves as one building block within the educational process. In that context, we try to provide a solution that is open and extendable for all parties involved.

Collaborative Environment

We find teacher-student interactions essential. Being able to work together to solve problems through a collaborative approach is something we try to encourage through our product.

Get in touch
